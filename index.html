sbt-no-publish - Setting No Publication Settings

Quick Start

To use sbt-no-publish in an existing SBT project with Scala 2.11 or a later version, add the following dependencies to your build.sbt depending on your needs:

libraryDependencies ++= Seq(
  "io.chrisdavenport" %% "sbt-no-publish" % "<version>"
)
